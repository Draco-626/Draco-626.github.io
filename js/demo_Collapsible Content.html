What We Do

Building For Better is a company which focuses on creating and providing homes for impoverished people living in extreme poverty. Each house has all the necessary facilites working such as plumbing, heating, and sufficent insulation.... Our homes provide a safe place for families to work and relax together, allowing people to focus on one less thing. We work to help those indeed find a safe place that they can own and call their 'home'. Each home is designed to be long lasting and durable. With multiple rooms for privacy, social, and work, Building For Better homes are here to add a sense of security and safety to those born into disadvantaged lives.
