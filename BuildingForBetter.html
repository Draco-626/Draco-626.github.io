What We Do

Building For Better is a company which focuses on creating and providing homes for impoverished people living in extreme poverty. Each house has all the necessary facilites working such as plumbing, heating, and sufficent insulation. Our homes provide a safe place for families to work and relax together, allowing people to focus on one less thing. We work to help those indeed find a safe place that they can own and call their 'home'.


Each home is designed to be long lasting and durable. With multiple rooms for privacy, social, and work, Building For Better homes are here to add a sense of security and safety to those born into disadvantaged lives.

Our Homes

Building For Better provides homes with working plumbing. this is a big step for those families who struggle to find clean water in wells and rivers.

Building For Better provides homes with sufficent insulation. Protection from harsh elements as well as warmth from harsh elements lowers the risk of illness and provides a sense of security.

Building For Better provides homes with with working electricity. This allows families to keep warm, have light during night time, and cook warm, nourishing food in their own homes.

Why We Do This

Building For Better is inspired by the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals, specifically Goal 1 - 'Ending Poverty In All Its Forms Everywhere.' The United Nations website states that roughly 700 million people (or 10% of the world's population) is living in extreme poverty. This means that individuals and families struggle to find access to basic needs such as education, food, shelter, water and so much more which many people take for granted. Building For Better works to provide at least some of these necessities. Furthermore, providing people homes guarantees struggling families a sense of stability and safety in their tragically unstable and potentially dangerous lives.
